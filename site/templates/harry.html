Harry's Body Recomp Nutrition Plan

Morning (10g)

  • Full shaker of warm water
  • 2 multivitamin capsules
  • 2 bowls of cereal
  • A coffee if you need one

Lunch (25g)

  • 3 large eggs on toast
  • Another shaker of warm water

Afternoon (>90g)

Dense smoothie. Remember to drink over the whole afternoon (spread out over 3-4 hours, your body can only process so much protein at a time) and leave at least half for after the gym.

  • 3 scoops protein powder
  • 1 scoop creatine
  • 1 scoop super greens
  • 500 ml egg whites
  • 200 ml prune juice
  • 1 shot of apple cider vinegar
  • Whatever else you want/can fit in the blender. I'd recommend any leafy greens or leftover veggies around the kitchen but for taste, two tablespoons of peanut butter, a banana and frozen berries go a long way.

Dinner (~80g)

  • 300 g of steak or chicken if you can get some, cooked however you like.
  • Whatever Susan has prepared BUT remove any pasta, rice, cereal or bread. Carbs in the evening will fuk you up. Strictly meat and veg.
  • One more shaker of warm water before bed or a glass of milk.
